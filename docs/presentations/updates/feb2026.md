# Status update - February 2026

Easysort focuses on 1 main thing: AI analysis of waste images. We then deploy and integrate this into various different applications. We provide various modules and serve 2 customer groups with somewhat tailored solutions, as we are in a stage of figuring out which modules provide value.

We democratize AI waste technology by making it open-source, private and transparent.

## Modules

### Municipality recycling centers

- **Waste recording:** Recording a recycling tent where people can put and get items for recycling that are too good to throw out. We record what items are being recycled and give that information to municipalities for reporting and optimizing these streams.
- **Waste guide:** A physical robot we have developed that you can place at a recycling center. It helps customers recycle by showing them which container items should go into, to limit impurities and specifically residual waste fractions.

### Waste transfer and sorting facilities

- **Overview of waste streams:** Various methods to view loads, purity and overview of waste streams and impurities at transfer stations. These modules are still in early development — figuring out what provides real value to operators.

## Values

### 1. Open-source

Most of our critical, non-production implementations are open-source. We want competitors to get inspiration, to drive down cost, to drive products and performance up. We don't believe AI for waste should be controlled by a singular company or just a few — it should be freely available to everyone.

### 2. Private

We distribute our models, software and most integrations freely, so everyone can set up our software by themselves. All our servers run in the EU, mostly on our own infrastructure and servers in Copenhagen.

### 3. Transparency

Everything we know is freely available on our docs. Most business communication is also open.

## Business model

We make money by implementing our software into your streams and providing service on those systems. This is cheaper than doing it yourself, but should we raise prices or go in a direction you don't like, you have the option to implement our systems yourself and become independent.

## Data & tracking

All data visualization and data is only available through our tracking service called EasyTrack. You can also set up your own tracking service. We provide many different views for various applications and an API for easy access to Power BI and other applications. We focus on providing systems that help operators better optimize and gain insights into how the facility is running.

## Why Easysort?

### Differentiation

We build one large foundation model for waste recognition. From that base, we or others can create specific models for any application — getting a deep understanding of fractions, waste types and purity. We are the only company doing this, and doing it open-source.

### Why now

AI vision models have reached a level where they can do a lot for waste. The waste industry is far behind on image AI adoption. This is the push forward that the industry needs.

### The problem (Denmark, 2022 data)

- Denmark produces ~21 million tons of waste (excl. dirt)
- 3.1 million tons of Danish waste goes to incineration, plus 532.000 tons imported
- Household waste: only 55% recycled, 43% burned
- Residual waste alone: 1.38 million tons
- Copenhagen municipality spends 800 mio. kr. on waste handling and sorting points
- EU pushed source separation onto citizens instead of funding infrastructure — Easysort is fixing this by making the technology cheap and available
